# KALNET Student Portal — User Guide

Welcome to the KALNET Student Portal! This guide will help you get started with the essential features of the portal.

## 1. Getting Started
To access your portal, click on the **Student Login** link.
*   **Username**: Your school email address.
*   **Password**: Your unique **Roll Number** (e.g., `KN-2024-001`).

## 2. Your Dashboard
Once you log in, you will see your Dashboard. It gives you a quick overview of:
*   **Fee Status**: How much is due and what you've already paid.
*   **Recent Activity**: The latest updates on your leave requests and school announcements.
*   **Quick Links**: Fast access to pay fees, request leave, or check notices.

## 3. Managing Fees
Click on **Fees** in the sidebar to view your detailed payment schedule.
*   You can see all terms (Tuition, Transport, Activity).
*   **Overdue** payments are highlighted in red.
*   Download receipts for payments you've already made.

## 4. Requesting Leave
Need to take a day off?
*   Go to the **Leave** section.
*   Fill in the **Leave Type**, **Dates**, and **Reason**.
*   Once submitted, your request is automatically sent to the school administration for approval. You can track the status in the Leave History table.

## 5. Announcements & Notices
Stay updated with everything happening at school.
*   Check the **Announcements** board for sports meets, exam schedules, and holidays.
*   Use categories like **Exams** or **Holidays** to filter the news.

## 6. Your Profile
Visit **My Profile** to verify your details, including your parent contact info and roll number. If you spot any errors, please contact the school office.

---
**Need Help?**
If you have trouble logging in or using the portal, please reach out to the **Technical Support Cell** in the computer lab.
